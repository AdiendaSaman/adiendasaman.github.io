Carbon AwarenessKesadaran Karbon

Personal carbon footprint trackerPelacak jejak karbon pribadi

Travel generates emissions. Some organisations monitor this directly. I track my own.Perjalanan menghasilkan emisi. Beberapa organisasi memantau ini secara langsung. Saya melacak milik saya sendiri.

Many companies — particularly those with sustainability mandates or net-zero commitments — now track travel emissions as part of their operational carbon accounting. This is one of the specific responsibilities listed in travel operations roles: "monitor trends, spend, emissions and compliance with company policy."Banyak perusahaan — khususnya yang memiliki mandat keberlanjutan atau komitmen net-zero — kini melacak emisi perjalanan sebagai bagian dari akuntansi karbon operasional mereka.

Why I track thisMengapa saya melacak ini

I completed training in carbon accounting through Rise Social and biodiversity finance through UNDP. Beyond the certificates, I apply this knowledge practically: my personal travel carbon tracker uses the distance-based emission factor method, with greenhouse gas conversion factors from the UK Department of Energy Security and Net Zero (DESNZ). This means each trip is converted to CO₂ equivalent based on transport mode and distance, not rough estimates. For organisations that monitor travel emissions as part of their sustainability commitments, this is exactly the kind of operational thinking that sits behind responsible travel policy.Saya menyelesaikan pelatihan carbon accounting melalui Rise Social dan biodiversity finance melalui UNDP. Di luar sertifikat, saya menerapkan pengetahuan ini secara praktis: pelacak karbon perjalanan pribadi saya menggunakan metode berbasis jarak, dengan faktor konversi gas rumah kaca dari Departemen Keamanan Energi dan Net Zero (DESNZ) Inggris. Setiap perjalanan dikonversi ke CO₂ ekuivalen berdasarkan mode transportasi dan jarak.
